## Conflict Check

- Cardiovascular additions (SCORE2 family) are consistently supportive across derivation and external-validation cohorts.
- Liver scores show stronger clinical breadth for ALBI than FLI/BARD in modern specialist pathways; FLI/BARD remain useful low-cost triage tools.
- Metabolic additions (METS-IR, TyG-BMI) are supported by large cohorts but mostly observational, so they are best used for risk stratification rather than diagnosis.
